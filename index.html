LATITUDE


This project is an investigation and exploration into the possible correlations between the dependent variables of weather, temperature, humidity, wind velocity, and cloud density in a given latitude. The "usual suspects", in terms of data science tools, are being used to effect the foray into these investigations, including pandas, matplotlib, and sklearn (pandas module). The data is being extracted from openweatherapi.org, a purveyor of geographical and climate related data.
